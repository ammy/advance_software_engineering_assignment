How To Use Pocketfriend App


Pocketfriend helps you to know yourself.


The App is about you, for you and by you. It relies on your input and needs your regular updates.


All the data you put in is anonymous: we don’t even know who you are!


It will reward you with insights in your own behaviour that you did not know existed. It will be a friend forever – a friend who never forgets what you shared, a friend that will help you enjoy good times more and support you through bad times. A friend in your pocket. 


And it will grow continuously as it gets to know you better. 


Remember though: only you can make this work through regular updates. So please use it often and please be honest and open.


Take some time, use it slowly, read all the bubbles and consider them and then try to answer all the questions deliberately and fully. The main point of the app is to get you to think about your emotions, and work out the things you do that make you feel better, and worse."

About Pocketfriend App


What  do you get when you cross a neuroscientist/psychologist with an entrepreneur/eternal optimist?


Pocketfriend!


Started at the footy field, Pocketfriend aims to help you live a happier life. It simply helps you pay attention to what behaviours make you happy and which don’t. 


We are an enthusiastic couple of friends that are trying to make a small difference by bringing a little bit more happiness into people’s lives. 


That’s it. 


Enjoy Pocketfriend!
